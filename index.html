About Us

Prasang Planners - The Socio Culture & Events Management Company was founded in 2024 by Maanil Shah, a 20-year-old visionary entrepreneur leading a dynamic team of 50+ professionals. We operate pan-India, delivering exceptional socio-culture productions and comprehensive event management services.

Our company comprises two specialized divisions: The Socio Culture for commercial productions including podcast shooting & editing, product photoshoots, advertisement shoots, social media management, content creation, and theatre productions; and Events Management providing end-to-end event solutions.

With our head office in Ahmedabad and a collaborating office in Mumbai (Radiance Studio, directed by our business partner Forum Shah), we combine creativity with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every project reflects your unique vision while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and execution.
